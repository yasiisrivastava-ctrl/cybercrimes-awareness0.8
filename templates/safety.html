Stay Safe Online

Small habits can protect you from most cyber attacks. Learn simple steps to keep yourself and your data secure.

🔐 Strong Passwords

Create unique passwords using letters, numbers and symbols.

🛡 Two-Factor Authentication

Enable 2FA to add an extra layer of security.

💳 Safe Online Payments

Always verify websites before making any payment.

📧 Email Safety

Never click suspicious links or download unknown attachments.

📱 Social Media Safety

Keep your personal information private and use strong privacy settings.

📶 Public Wi-Fi

Avoid banking or shopping when connected to public Wi-Fi.
